About Cynthia

Bio

Cynthia Abah is a Software Engineer with primary interest in web development. She currently works with modern technologies like React, Next.js, and Tailwind CSS to build intuitive and responsive web applications. Cynthia is also exploring backend development basics with Node.js and Django, authentication systems, and APIs to strengthen her understanding of scalable software design.

As a Health Enthusiast with a strong analytical mindset, she brings a problem-solving approach rooted in structure, empathy, and precision. Her long-term goal is to merge technology and healthcare through innovative software solutions that improve access, efficiency, and user experience.
